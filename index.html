Homemade Meat Lover's Pizza

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 5-6
Ingredients
1/3 stick butter
1 package pepperoni
1 lb italian sausage
2 cups of cheese
1/4 cup Alfredo sauce
1 can Pizza Crust
4 tbls parm. cheese

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.
Use stick of butter to grease the pan you will be using.
Place pizza crust in oven for 13 minutes
layer with ingredients as you like
put in oven for 14-17 minutes
let cool off and enjoy!

Pecan Overload Cake

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 12
Ingredients
Betty Crocker™ Super Moist™ Delights Butter Pecan Cake Mix,
and all ingredients listed on back of box
as much crushed pecans as you want
1 can coconut pecan icing

Preheat oven accordingly and prepare cake mix as
directed, adding as many pecans as you want to the cake batter
Bake as directed on back of box
Let set for about 5 minutes and enjoy!
